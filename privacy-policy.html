Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our apps distributed through the Apple App Store.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on the features you use, we may collect:

  • Account information: such as email address, display name, and login credentials.
  • Purchase data: transaction status for in-app purchases and subscriptions (for example, active/expired status).
  • User content: photos and other files you upload for app features.
  • Usage and device data: app diagnostics, crash logs, basic device/app version information.
  • Locally stored data: preferences, cached files, and settings saved on your device.
  • Cookie or similar identifier data: where web views or web-based components are used.
  • AI interaction data: prompts, generated outputs, and feedback submitted through AI features.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain app functionality.
  • Enable account creation, sign-in, and account security.
  • Process and validate in-app purchases and subscription access.
  • Store and display uploaded images and related user content.
  • Improve product quality, including AI feature quality and safety.
  • Detect fraud, abuse, and technical issues.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

Payments are processed by Apple, not by us directly. We may receive subscription status and transaction metadata necessary to provide paid features and customer support.

4. Local Storage

Some data is stored locally on your device for performance and offline functionality. You can remove local app data by deleting app data or uninstalling the app, subject to your device settings.

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

If the app includes web-based content, cookies or similar technologies may be used for authentication, feature operation, and analytics. You can manage cookie settings through your device/browser controls where available.

6. Account Creation and Management

If account features are enabled, we process your account data to provide login, profile, and support services. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.

7. Photo Upload and Storage

Photos you upload may be stored on secure storage providers to deliver core app functionality. Please avoid uploading content you do not have rights to use.

8. Use of AI Features

Our apps may include AI-powered features. AI output can be inaccurate or incomplete. You should independently review AI-generated content before relying on it. We may use AI interaction data to improve quality and prevent misuse.

9. Data Sharing

We may share data with:

  • Service providers (hosting, analytics, customer support, storage, AI processing).
  • Payment platform partners as required for transaction validation.
  • Authorities where required by law or legal process.

10. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide services, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Retention duration varies by data type and legal requirements.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. To submit a request, contact us through the support channel below.

12. Children

Our apps are not intended for children under the age required by applicable law unless explicitly stated otherwise. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us so we can take appropriate action.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material updates will be reflected by changing the "Last updated" date.

14. Contact

For privacy or data requests, visit our Support Page. You can also review User Privacy and our EULA.
