User Privacy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

This page summarizes your privacy choices and rights when using our apps. For complete details, please review our Privacy Policy.

1. Data You Provide

You may provide account details, content uploads (including photos), support requests, and optional information during app usage.

2. Billing and Subscription Data

In-app purchases and subscriptions are handled by Apple. We receive limited purchase status information needed to enable paid features and provide support.

3. Local Data Storage

The app may store settings and cached content locally on your device. Removing local app data through your device settings may clear this information.

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

If our app uses web-based components, cookies or similar technologies may be used for session management, functionality, and analytics.

5. Account Creation and Deletion

If your app account is enabled, you can request updates or deletion of account-related personal data by contacting support.

6. Photo Upload and Storage

Uploaded images may be stored on cloud infrastructure to provide product features. You should only upload content you own or are authorized to use.

7. AI Features

AI interactions may be processed to return requested outputs and improve quality/safety. AI-generated content may be incorrect and should be reviewed by you before use.

8. Your Rights and Requests

Subject to applicable law, you may request access, correction, deletion, or portability of personal data. Contact support to submit a request.

9. Contact

For privacy requests, visit our Customer Support page. You can also review our EULA and Terms of Use.
